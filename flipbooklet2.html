COMMUNICATIVE STRATEGIES

Listening & Hearing, there is a huge difference between these two strategies of communication, even with their similarities. These communication strategies are essential for daily use, as it will help make communication much easier and to lessen communication problems.

listening

is to listen with the intention of paying attention to the conversation. Listening while also understanding everything in the conversation.

hearing

is the process of being able to “hear” anything or anyone make noises. Hearing is also part of your 5 senses, in which tones and noise are received by stimuli.
